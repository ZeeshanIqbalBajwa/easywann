SASE Everywhere

easyWAN is the modern secure network.

Available via the cloud, on-premises, or as a blended combination of both, easyWAN SASE connects Enterprise branches, teleworkers, and end users securely and reliably to applications in the cloud or data centers around the world.

Read More

SASE is a significantly differentiated solution enabled through unique software, management, pricing, and the ability to deploy in the cloud, on-premises, or hosted.

One Software Stack

A single-pipeline integrated architecture combines comprehensive security, advanced networking, industry-leading SD-WAN, robust analytics, and simplified automation into one software solution.

Single Management Interface

Consistent enforcement of security and networking policies to anywhere in the world with a single, easy-to-use management interface hosted on-premises or in the cloud.

Disruptive Pricing Model

No hidden costs, no additional products, no painful integrations, no unnecessary appliances to get the most robust suite of SASE services on the market.

Deploy Anywhere

Whether in the cloud, on-premises, or as a blended combination of both, easyWAN can satisfy any brownfield and multi-cloud requirements while scaling to tens of thousands of branch sites.
